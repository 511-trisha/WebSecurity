About Us

We are a team of technologically inspired minds that envisage great advancement in the fields of Computer Science and IT. Our goals are to:

  • Learn about innovative Web security products and programs.
  • Create websites.
  • Collaborate and test the security measures in each website
  • Identify the various online attacks
  • Try and test the websites using those attacks!
